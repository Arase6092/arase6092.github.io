# 📽️ textwavedeck - Professional presentations with smooth cinematic navigation

[![Download Version 1.0.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Arase6092/textwavedeck)

WaveDeck turns your Windows computer into a professional stage for PowerPoint presentations. It offers high-quality export options and intuitive controls. You manage your slides with fluid transitions and clear visual previews.

## 🛠️ System Requirements

Ensure your computer meets these requirements before you start:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Processor: Intel Core i5 or equivalent.
*   Memory: 8 GB RAM.
*   Storage: 200 MB of free space.
*   Software: Microsoft PowerPoint installed on your machine.

## 🌟 Key Features

WaveDeck simplifies the way you present information to an audience.

*   **Local-First Design:** Your data stays on your machine. You do not need an internet connection to run your presentations.
*   **4K Export:** Save your slides in high resolution for large screens.
*   **Thumbnail Preview:** View your slide deck layout before you present.
*   **Cinematic Carousel:** Navigate between slides with smooth animations that keep your audience engaged.
*   **Gesture-Ready Architecture:** Use touch-enabled devices or gesture hardware to control your slides.

## ⚙️ How to use the application

### Loading a presentation
Open the application and click the "Open PPTX" button. Select your PowerPoint file from your file explorer. The application loads your slides into the carousel view automatically.

### Navigating slides
Use your mouse wheel, the left and right arrow keys, or a connected gesture device to move through your deck. The carousel provides a preview of upcoming slides to help you stay on track.

### Exporting slides
Select "Export" from the top menu to save your current view. You can choose different quality settings, including 4K resolution, to ensure your materials look crisp on high-end monitors.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this app work with older PowerPoint files?**
Yes, the software supports standard PPTX files created in modern versions of Microsoft Office.

**Can I use a remote clicker?**
Yes, the application recognizes standard input commands from USB clickers and remotes that mimic keyboard arrow inputs.

**Where does the software save my files?**
The software does not upload files. It works with local files on your hard drive to ensure privacy.

**What should I do if the application crashes?**
Close the application, check for Windows updates, and restart the software. Ensure you have the latest version of the application from the download link.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during installation or use, check these common items:

*   **Permissions:** You may need administrator rights to install the software on certain corporate computers.
*   **File Format:** Ensure your PowerPoint files are not password-protected by external security software, as this may block the preview renderer.
*   **Display Settings:** If the 4K export looks small, verify that your Windows display scale settings are set to 100%.
